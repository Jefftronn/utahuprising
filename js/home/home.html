We are a competitive paintball team based out of the Salt Lake City area in Utah. We currently play Division 4 and plan on focusing all of our time in the West Coast Paintball Player League. We are very interested in playing in the NXL and also close by state events for the 2016 season.

We also hold on-going tryouts throughout the year to give players a chance to join this organization. Please contact us to inquire about this. We plan on seeing you guys in the 2016 season.

Team Blog

It all started with a couple of people who decided to start a competative team. Magic and Roby first started to look for talent in paintball fields throughout the Salt Lake Valley.

They hand picked a few players to join, while most of these players have never played together. We then grew together and became family with one goal in mind, to win!

Our mission is to grow, learn, and challenge others and ourselves by playing at the highest level of paintball.

We definitely want to see the Utah paintball scene grow, and be part of this already huge community. Please let us know if you need help with anything related to paintball.
